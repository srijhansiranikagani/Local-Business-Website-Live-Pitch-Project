Our Story

A kitchen built on flame, family, and patience.

Saffron & Smoke began in a tiny lane in Old Delhi — three generations, one tandoor, and a single rule: never rush the spice. Today, we plate that same fire in a setting designed for slow, unforgettable evenings.
